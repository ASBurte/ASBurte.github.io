Welcome to CatTestHub!

A collection of experimental chemical information over catalytic surfaces. The CPEC chemical catalysis database was designed to house experimentally measured kinetic, thermodynamic, and characterization information relevant to chemical turnover on catalytic surfaces. Below you can select from a growing list of catalytic chemistries that have been experimentally measured by CPEC members or curated from the scientific literature.
